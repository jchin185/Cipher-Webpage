  • Caesar Cipher

    This simple cipher shifts each letter a certain amount.

    Example: Shift 1

    ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
    BCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZA

    Example: Shift -5

    ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ
    VWXYZABCDEFHGIJKLMNOPQRSLTU

    NOTE: Shifts greater than 25 or less than -25 are the same as the remainder of that number divided by 25.

    A shift of 26 is the same as a shift of 1.
